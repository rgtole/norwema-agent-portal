## 🛠️ Recommended Websites for WebP to MP4 Conversion & Audio Overlays

Since WebP is an image format, you need to convert it to a video format (like MP4) and add your voice recording. Here are the best free online tools to do this:

### 1. Canva (Recommended)
*   **Link:** [Canva Video Editor](https://www.canva.com/)
*   **How to do it:** 
    1. Click "Create a design" -> "Video".
    2. Upload the `agent_walkthrough_1783377818150.webp` file to your uploads.
    3. Drag it onto the canvas.
    4. Use Canva's built-in record tool ("Uploads" -> "Record yourself") to record your voice while watching the video play, or upload a pre-recorded audio file.
    5. Download the final project as an **MP4 Video**.

### 2. EZGIF
*   **Link:** [EZGIF WebP to MP4 Converter](https://ezgif.com/webp-to-mp4)
*   **How to do it:**
    1. Upload the `agent_walkthrough_1783377818150.webp` file.
    2. Click **Convert WebP to MP4** and download the silent video file.
    3. You can then use EZGIF's "Add Audio to Video" tool or use any editor below to overlay your voice-over.

### 3. Kapwing
*   **Link:** [Kapwing Video Editor](https://www.kapwing.com/)
*   **How to do it:**
    1. Upload the WebP file directly into a new project (it will treat it as a video).
    2. Click **Record** and select **Audio** to record your voice over the timeline.
    3. Export the project as an MP4.

### 4. Adobe Express
*   **Link:** [Adobe Express Video Editor](https://www.adobe.com/express/feature/video/editor)
*   **How to do it:**
    1. Start a new video project.
    2. Upload the WebP, add your voice-over track, and export.
